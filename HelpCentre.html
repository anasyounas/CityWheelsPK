Frequently Asked Questions


How do I book a ride?
Select a pickup location, choose your ride type, and confirm your driver.
What if I have an issue during the ride?
You can message or call the driver via app, or reach support at +92 336 1061946.
Can I cancel a ride?
Yes, cancellation is free within the first 5 minutes; otherwise a small fee may apply.

Still need help?

Reach out to our support team via email or phone—we’re available 24/7.
