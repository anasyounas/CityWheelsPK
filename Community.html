Community Guidelines

Creating a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone

Respect and Safety

We expect all riders and drivers to treat one another with courtesy and respect. Harassment, hate speech, and discrimination are strictly prohibited.

Trust and Integrity

Never misrepresent yourself or engage in fraudulent behavior. Transparency and honesty are imperative to maintain a trustworthy community.

Accountability

Please report any unsafe or disrespectful behavior immediately via our app or email at support@citywheelspk.com. Violations may result in account suspension.
