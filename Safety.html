Safety First

How CityWheelsPK protects passengers and drivers

Driver Vetting

All drivers undergo background checks and in-person interviews before being approved to drive with us.


In-Ride Safety Features

  • Real-time Tracking: Family or friends can follow the ride via shared link.
  • Emergency Button: Instantly connect to support 24/7.
  • Ratings System: Passengers and drivers rate each other after each ride.

Health & Hygiene

All vehicles are required to be cleaned daily, and drivers must follow standard health protocols.
