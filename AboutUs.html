Who We Are

CityWheelsPK is a tech-driven ride-sharing platform designed to connect people across cities with ease and comfort. We’re here to solve transportation challenges by bringing smart, seamless, and affordable mobility to every doorstep.

Our Mission

We strive to empower commuters by providing safe, efficient, and cost-effective travel options. Our mission is to simplify daily transport while promoting sustainability and customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

We envision a future where transportation in Pakistan is smarter, cleaner, and accessible to all — whether you’re a passenger heading to work or a driver looking to earn with flexibility and respect.
