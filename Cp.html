How we use cookies to improve your experience

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your device to help us remember preferences and analyze site usage.

How We Use Them


  • Essential: Needed for secure login and navigation.
  • Analytics: We track anonymous usage data to improve the service.
  • Preferences: Cookies store settings like language and login status.

Your Choices

You can disable cookies in your browser, but some parts of the website might not work properly.
